Privacy Policy

Aonic Content — Last updated June 2026

1. What We Collect

When you connect your TikTok account, we receive an access token that allows us to upload video drafts to your TikTok inbox on your behalf. We also store your TikTok user ID to identify your account. We do not collect passwords, payment information, or any personal data beyond what is necessary to operate the service.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your TikTok access token is used solely to upload video content to your TikTok inbox for your review. No content is published publicly without your explicit approval inside the TikTok app. We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, or any purpose beyond delivering the service.

3. Data Storage

Access tokens are encrypted at rest and stored in a secure database. They are never shared with third parties, sold, or used for any purpose other than authenticating requests to the TikTok API on your behalf.

4. Data Retention

We retain your access token and associated account data for as long as you use the service. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us or by revoking access through TikTok's app settings under Manage App Permissions.

5. Third-Party Services

This app integrates with the TikTok API. Your use of TikTok is governed by TikTok's Privacy Policy. We are not responsible for TikTok's data practices.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.
